### FAQ (5 questions localises et optimises pour le marche anglophone)

**Q: Do I need to own a guitar before starting?**
A: We recommend having any standard acoustic or electric guitar before starting. The course is designed to work with any beginner guitar — you don't need an expensive instrument to get started.

**Q: I have never played music before. Will I be able to follow this course?**
A: Yes, this is exactly who this course is built for. You start from the very first chord with clear video explanations. No sheet music, no music theory required to begin.

**Q: How is this different from free YouTube tutorials?**
A: YouTube tutorials are scattered and inconsistent. This course gives you a structured progression — each lesson builds on the last — so you make real, measurable progress instead of jumping randomly between videos.

**Q: How long will it take to see results?**
A: Most students play their first song within 4 to 8 weeks of regular 20-30 minute practice sessions. The method eliminates the most common beginner mistakes that slow progress.

**Q: Can I access the course if I'm not based in France?**
A: Absolutely. The course is fully online and available worldwide. Lessons are in French, but the on-screen demonstrations make them accessible even for non-French speakers. English subtitles are on the roadmap.
